Logic Circuit Builder

Drag and drop nodes to build custom logic circuits

How to Build a Circuit

  1. Add Nodes: Click the buttons in the toolbar to spawn Input nodes, Logic Gates, or Output bulbs.
  2. Connect: Click and drag from an Output port (right side) to an Input port (left side) to wire them together.
  3. Simulate: Click on the circular Input nodes to toggle them between 0 and 1. The circuit will evaluate automatically!
